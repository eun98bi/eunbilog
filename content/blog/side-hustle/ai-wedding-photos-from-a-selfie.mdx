I've been building a small side project called [yourweddingai.com](https://yourweddingai.com), and I want to share why I made it and how it actually works.

## The problem I wanted to solve

A real wedding photoshoot costs a lot of money and takes a lot of planning — studio booking, hair and makeup, outfits, a photographer's schedule. Not everyone wants (or can afford) all of that just to get a few nice wedding-style photos.

So I started wondering: what if you could just upload a selfie and get a wedding photoshoot back, generated by AI?

## What yourweddingai.com actually does

It's simple. You upload a selfie, the AI generates a wedding-style photo of you in full wedding outfit, styling, and setting. No studio, no scheduling, no waiting weeks for edited photos.

When you sign up, you get **2 free coins**, so you can try it out before spending anything.

## How it works, step by step

1. Go to [yourweddingai.com](https://yourweddingai.com) and create an account
2. Upload a clear selfie (a regular front-facing photo works fine)
3. Let the AI generate your wedding photo
4. Download the result

- Couples who want wedding-style photos without booking a studio
- People who need something quick for invitations or social media
- Anyone curious about what an AI-generated wedding photo even looks like

If you want to test it, head to [yourweddingai.com](https://yourweddingai.com), sign up, and use your 2 free coins on a selfie of your own. It only takes a couple of minutes from upload to result.
